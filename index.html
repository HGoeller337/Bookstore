Featured Review

Fahrenheit 451

By Riveting Reads user Barbara Jackson

Easily one of the most influential and important books written to date. I highly recommend giving it a read, because many of its core themes have only become more relevant as time goes on. If you are looking for a book that will make you think about the state of the world today, definitely give this one a try.
